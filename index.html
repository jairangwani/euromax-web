About Euromax

Two decades supplying the UAE's networks

Founded in 2001, Euromax has grown from a Dubai-based electronics and IT-hardware trader into one of the UAE's trusted names in networking and structured cabling. For over twenty years we have supplied contractors, system integrators, and enterprises across the Emirates with reliable copper and fibre-optic cabling infrastructure.

Our strength lies in a deep local distribution network combined with direct, long-standing relationships with leading manufacturers. Every product we supply is selected to meet international performance standards and UAE regulatory requirements — backed by in-house technical expertise and a commitment to quality that has kept our clients returning for more than two decades.
